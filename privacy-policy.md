# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-17

This Privacy Policy explains how Diary255 ("we", "us", or "our") handles your data when you use the Diary255 application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that you retain full control over your data.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage
**We do not collect, track, transmit, or store any personal user details on any remote servers.** 

All data generated by the App—including but not limited to text, images, estimates, location data, and app settings—remains exclusively on your local device. The data is stored locally in a SQLite database and within local files on your device. We do not have access to this data.

## 2. Device Permissions and Sensor Data
To provide core functionalities, the App may request certain device permissions:
* **Location:** Used to automate travel tracking and geofencing between work sites. This data is strictly processed on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
* **Camera & Photo Library:** Used to capture and attach images to your diary entries, or for local image processing (e.g., ML Kit text/barcode recognition). All image processing occurs strictly on your device.
* **Microphone / Speech Recognition:** Used for speech-to-text dictation features. Audio processing relies on your device's built-in APIs and is not transmitted to us.

We only request these permissions to enable the App's features. We do not monetize, harvest, or transmit your sensor data.

## 3. Third-Party Services and Purchases
The only external data tracking related to our App concerns purchases made through official app stores (e.g., Apple App Store, Google Play Store). When you purchase the App or make in-app purchases, those transactions are processed entirely by the respective app store. We do not receive, collect, or store your payment information. The app stores may provide us with anonymous, aggregated purchase data, but this data cannot be used to identify you personally.

## 4. Marketplace & Network Activity
The App includes a marketplace feature allowing you to browse and download configuration files (such as profiles, tools, and events). Accessing this marketplace requires an internet connection and will transmit standard, anonymous network information (such as your IP address and device model) to our servers or hosting providers to facilitate the download. **We do not upload, sync, or transmit any of your local diary entries, estimates, or personal data during this process.**

## 5. Sharing Data and Exporting
The App allows you to export your data (such as custom tools, profiles, reports, and images) and share it with others using your device's native sharing capabilities (e.g., email, SMS, WhatsApp, or other third-party apps). When you choose to share data in this manner, the App simply hands the data over to the selected third-party service. We do not monitor, intercept, or store the contents of what you share. Please be aware that once your data is shared via these third-party services, their respective privacy policies apply, and we are not responsible for how they handle your data.

## 6. User Rights and Data Deletion
Because your data remains strictly on your device, you have total control over it. You can access, edit, or permanently delete your data at any time directly through the App. **To execute a complete data deletion, you simply need to uninstall the App or clear the App's local storage data from your device settings.** Once deleted locally, the data cannot be recovered by us.

## 7. Data Security
The security of your data relies heavily on the security of your device. We strongly recommend using device-level security measures, such as passcodes or biometric authentication, to protect your information from unauthorized physical access. 

## 8. Children's Privacy
Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.

## 10. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us.
